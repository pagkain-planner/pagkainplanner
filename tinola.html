RECIPE

TINOLANG MANOK


SOURCE: https://www.kawalingpinoy.com/wp-content/
uploads/2013/03/chicken-tinola-4-730x973.jpg

BENEFITS

• Excellent source of protein – Protein is composed of amino acids. Our muscles rely on amino acids in order to build, repair, and develop.

• Source of Vitamin B3 – Vitamin B3 keeps our body healthy by improving cholesterol levels. It can also help improve our cardiovascular health.

• Stimulates the appetite – I think that tasty and delicious dishes such as tinolang manok or chicken tinola will automatically trigger your appetite the moment that you smell its aroma.


RECIPE

SERVINGS: 4

INGREDIENTS

1 TBSP cooking oil
1 red onion, small -sized, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 thumb sized ginger, cut into strips
2 TBSP patis (fish sauce)
1/2 kilo chicken, cut into pieces
1 liter water
1 small -sized green papaya, sliced
2 stalks dahon ng sili (sili leaves)


PROCEDURE

1. Sauté the onion, ginger, and garlic in cooking oil.

2. Add chicken and patis (fish sauce). let cook for a few minutes until chicken renders a bit of oil.

3. Add water and let boil.

4. When boiling, add papaya simmer until cooked.

5. Add dahon ng sili at the last minute and turn off heat.

6. Serve your chicken tinola while hot!
