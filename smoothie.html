RECIPE

REALLY GREEN SMOOTHIE


SOURCE: https://www.healthyseasonalrecipes.com/
wp-content/uploads/2020/06/healthy-green-smoothie-SQ-009-320x320.jpg

BENEFITS

• Rich with chlorophyll which some natural health experts say enhances the immune system, purifies the blood and rejuvenates the body.

• Full of antioxidants and carotenoids, which boost your brainpower, and help protect your brain.

• They are also full of B-vitamins, which are proven to help your memory, focus, and overall brain health and function.

• Contains fiber - Fiber is essential for good colon health and it keeps your bowels in working order.


RECIPE

SERVINGS: 1

INGREDIENTS

1 large ripe banana
1 cup packed baby kale or coarsely chopped mature kale
1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
¼ ripe avocado
1 tablespoon chia seeds
2 teaspoons honey
1 cup ice cubes


PROCEDURE

Combine banana, kale, almond milk, avocado, chia seeds and honey in a blender. Blend on high until creamy and smooth. Add ice and blend until smooth.
